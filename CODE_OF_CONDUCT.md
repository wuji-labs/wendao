# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), version 2.1** as its code of conduct.

We are committed to making participation in the Wendao community a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported confidentially to the maintainers at **conduct@wuji-labs.org**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and the privacy and security of the reporter will be respected.
